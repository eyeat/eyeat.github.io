Our Influence

NYC

As a New Yorkers, it is make sense that we try to come up with a solution to our city's problem.

Population

After doing some research, we found out that 60,000 NYC residents are blind with 360,000 of people are visually impaired

Insight

Together we want to help people with visual impairment to be more independent especially when they are in a restaurant seeting where often, they require assistance when they order food in restaurant

Our Journey

Brainstorm

Our team was formed during CUNY Hackathon Spring 2018. Coming from different colleges and expertises, we agreed on using the IBM Watson Technologies to make our idea into reality.

Planning

As it is inneficient to get each restaurants' information, we implement NYC Open Data API to get information from the restaurant nearby the users

Execution

After all of the items coming together, we start building our app using React Native framework to leverage our code reusability.
